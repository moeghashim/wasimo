Tournament Details

  • 16 teams, with 2 players per team.
  • Team play format for Saudi Hand.
  • Spots are first come, first served.
  • After 9 complete rounds, each team's combined score becomes the final team score. The team with the lower score wins.

Going Down

  • 71 points is the minimum required to go down, then it progresses for each player who wants to go down.
  • If you go down with less than the required total, your hand is dead.
  • You may go down after your second pull.
  • You can use another player's joker and finish your cards regardless of your total.

Dead Hands and Redeals

  • If your hand is dead, you keep your cards, keep playing regularly, and the next player can pull a card you throw.
  • Anytime you have more or less than 14 cards, your hand is dead and considered not down.
  • If you have 4 doubles, or 3 doubles and a joker, only after your first pull you may ask for a redeal.
  • If there is a fresh tie, the same player deals again.

Jokers, Conduct, and Officials

  • Joker is joker: fully wild, with no restrictions unless agreed before play.
  • If a joker is exposed during a cut misdeal, the player who receives the joker keeps it.
  • You must mention when you have only 3 cards and when you have only 1 card.
  • No signaling, verbal assistance, hints, or bystander influence is allowed.
  • Disputes are resolved by the official, whose decision is final.

How it works

  1. Sign up — the first 16 teams to confirm are in.
  2. Random draw — bracket is generated automatically.
  3. Round of 16 — best of 3.
  4. Quarterfinals → Final — single game knockout.
